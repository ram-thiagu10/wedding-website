Getting There

Venues at a Glance

ENGAGEMENT & WEDDING

Mangalya Auditorium

Chottanikara, Ernakulam, Kerala

Nestled right behind the sacred Chottanikkara Bhagavathy Temple in Ernakulam district, Mangalya Auditorium offers a serene and auspicious setting for the ceremonies.

HOW TO REACH THE VENUE

✈️ From Airport: ~38 km away via Seaport-Airport Road (1 hr 15 mins). Alternatively, take a feeder bus to Aluva Metro, ride to Tripunithura Station, then take a 15-minute auto.
🚂 From Ernakulam Junction (South): ~18 km away. Take a direct taxi/auto via Vyttila (40 mins), or take the Kochi Metro directly to Tripunithura Station followed by a short auto ride.
🚌 From Bus Stands: Direct local and KSRTC buses run frequently to Chottanikkara from Vyttila Mobility Hub and Kaloor Private Bus Stand.
🚗 By Road: From North, use Seaport-Airport Road via Thiruvankulam. From South, exit Kochi Bypass at Kundannoor Junction and proceed through Tripunithura.

RECEPTION

Ramyas Hotel

Trichy (Tiruchirappalli), Tamil Nadu

Ramyas Hotel is one of Trichy's most celebrated hospitality landmarks, centrally located in the heart of the city. Renowned for its warm service and elegant banquet facilities, it makes for a perfect venue to celebrate and welcome the newlyweds into their new chapter.

HOW TO REACH THE VENUE

✈️ From Airport: ~5.5 km from Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ). A direct taxi or auto via the Airport Road takes roughly 15 to 20 minutes.
🚂 From Trichy Railway Junction: ~1 km away. Located just a 5-minute drive or walk down Williams Road. Readily accessible by auto-rickshaws stationed outside the terminal.
🚌 From Bus Stands: Diagonally opposite the Trichy Central Bus Stand (less than a 3-minute walk). Highly convenient for guests arriving via long-distance government or private buses.
🚗 By Road: Centrally positioned in Cantonment. Easily accessed via the Chennai-Trichy Highway (NH 45). Direct entry via Williams Road with dedicated guest parking spaces on-site.
